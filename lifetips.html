Liam’s 27 Life Tips

Dear Liam,

Here are some life tips to continuously remember.

  • Happiness is within and unconditional. Every time you fall into the trap of thinking that X, Y, Z will bring you happiness – notice how you suffer.
  • Your body can heal itself. Don’t underestimate it’s power.
  • There’s nowhere to go. No one to be. Stop running away from who you are.
  • Love unconditionally. Give unconditionally.
  • Lean into the uncomfortable. Learn to enjoy the uncomfortable and you will reach a state of equanimity with all things in life.
  • Multiple full body orgasms are possible. Yes, for men too.
  • Family is the greatest place to practice meditation.

“If you want to check whether you are enlightened, go and live with your family.” – Ram Gojal

  • Hug your parents
  • Hug everyone
  • Don’t give your blue ball away. Read Lessons I Learned in Tamera (TBC)
  • Speak your mind.
  • Habits are king. Cultivate them now.

“The best time to plant a tree was 20 years ago. The second best time is now.” – Chinese Proverb

  • Meditate.
  • Meditate.
  • Meditate.
  • Be of service.

“We can do no great things, only small things with great love.” – Mother Teresa

  • Don’t drink fizzy drinks. Don’t consume sugar.
  • Squat when you poop.
  • Don’t take life so seriously.
  • Take life seriously.
  • Don’t watch porn. Read www.yourbrainonporn.com
  • Ask questions.
  • Listen with your whole being.
  • If it takes less than five minutes, do it now.
  • If you need help  – ask!
  • Contemplate death.

Five Regrets of the Dying:

1. I wish I’d had the courage to live a life true to myself, not the life others expected of me.

2. I wish I hadn’t worked so hard.

3. I wish I’d had the courage to express my feelings.

4. I wish I had stayed in touch with my friends.

5. I wish that I had let myself be happier.
