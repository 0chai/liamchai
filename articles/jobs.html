Yogi Job Description

This is a sample job description for a yogi. Currently there are many unfilled positions for yogis, bodhisattvas and buddhas. Please email sentientbeings@suffering.org for a job application.

Yogi Job Responsibilities:

Yogi Job Duties:

  • Reports to his or her chosen guru.
  • Accomplishes single-pointed concentration (shamatha) in order to make reliable and accurate observations of the mind.
  • Applies the single-pointed concentration to gain irreversible insights into the nature of reality.
  • Maintains the precepts that he or she has undertaken, these could be lay precepts or monastic precepts.
  • To renounce the eight mundane concerns.
  • To move lucidly through the waking, sleeping, dying and rebirth processes.
  • Offers prayers of supplications, prostrations and mandalas.
  • Equalises self and other.

Yogi Skills and Qualifications:

Be physiologically and mentally fit. The ideal candidate would have achieved shamatha or be dedicating time to achieving it. It is a requirement to have aspiring bodhichitta.
