COVID-19 and a Duck

This graph below was from an article dated 4th March 2020. The source data is here.

It makes COVID-19 look scary. The data lines up with what the WHO said on 3rd March that the fatality rate for COVID-19 was 3.4%.

Yet the latest figures from the Centre for Disease Control (CDC) for COVID-19 Case Fatality Ratio (CFR) shows these figures from the following age groups:

  • 0-49 years old = .05%
  • 50-64 years old = .2%
  • 65+ years old = 1.3%
  • Overall ages = .4%

Note the data they use is for Symptomatic CFR. Two columns down they estimate that asymptomatic infections are 35%. It means the truer CFR is likely 35% less.

This gives these new figures:

  • 0-49 years old = .03%
  • 50-64 years old = .13%
  • 65+ years old = .85%
  • Overall ages = .26%

Now compare these figures to the seasonal flu figures:

  • 0-49 years old = .01%
  • 50-64 years old = .06%
  • 65+ years old = .83%
  • Overall ages = .10%

So COVID-19 is more fatal than the seasonal flu, but not by much. Compared to what the WHO or Dr. Anthony Fauci estimated of 3.4% and 2%, these current figures are a blib.

If it looks like a duck, swims like a duck, and quacks like a duck… it’s probably the seasonal flu a duck.

The Unseen

If it does turn out that COVID-19 is no where near as fatal as some organisations have projected, what are the unseen effects of lockdown policies on countries?

The UN already predicts coronavirus lockdown policies could push 130 million to the brink of starvation by the end of 2020. NGO World Vision warns that 30 million children are at risk of dying.

For every life saved from COVID-19 because of lockdown policies like social distancing or wearing masks, was there a death of despair caused by it?

If we save the life of a 75 year old today. Do we take the life of a 41-year old by suicide because he cannot afford to pay his rent?

For every 83 year old we manage to give a few extra months of life to, how many 16 year olds do we rob of crucial periods to study and learn at school? How many 11-year olds do we mentally scar and paint the world as a scary and dangerous place – full of germs waiting to kill you?
